Full Measure

Community watchdog app that allows users to report the locations of malfeasance* and attach images to markers.

* Malfeasance referres to dog poop in this context

What is Full Measure?

See Something Say Something

My Partner, Rob Kondratowicz, and I developed an application for users to report sightings of dog poop in their neighborhood. A user adds an address, description, and image to their post and it's added to an index of reports in your area.
